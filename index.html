Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Biostatistics at the Centre for Quantitative Medicine of Duke-NUS Medical School (Singapore). I am part of the research lab headed by Dr Bibhas Charkraborty, on developing and improving the statistical methodology for adaptive designs, such as the sequential multiple assignment randomized trials (SMARTs) and microrandomized trials (MRTs). I am also actively collaborating with Dr Cui YiFan on theoretical reinforcement learning works and applied projects.
